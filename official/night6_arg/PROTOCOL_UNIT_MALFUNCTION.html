> PROTOCOL // UNIT_MALFUNCTION — STANDARD RESPONSE

> STEP 1: IDENTIFICATION

Monitor camera feeds at regular intervals. Identify which UNIT has deviated from assigned position. Note current location and movement trajectory.

> STEP 2: ASSESSMENT

Determine approach vector and proximity to control room. If UNIT is within 2 rooms of operator position, escalate to STEP 3 immediately.

> STEP 3: CONTAINMENT

Deploy physical barrier (shutter) on the affected access point. Maintain barrier until UNIT retreats to a safe distance (minimum 3 rooms from operator).

> STEP 4: MONITORING

Resume camera rotation. Confirm UNIT has returned to default patrol route. Log incident with timestamp.

> NOTES

- Physical barriers have limited power supply. Do not maintain indefinitely.

- Camera checks do not consume power but require manual cycling.

- Multiple simultaneous malfunctions may require priority triage.

- UNIT-01 requires additional clock winding protocol (see SPEC_UNIT_01).
